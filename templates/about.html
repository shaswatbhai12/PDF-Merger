About PDF Merger

A simple and powerful tool to merge multiple PDF files into one document.

Features

  • Drag & drop PDF files
  • Reorder files before merging
  • Modern black & white interface
  • Fast and secure processing

How to Use

  1. Upload PDF files by dragging or clicking
  2. Reorder files by dragging them
  3. Click "Merge PDFs" to combine
  4. Download your merged document
